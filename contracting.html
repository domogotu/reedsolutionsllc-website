Contracting Approach

Reeds Solutions, LLC operates as a prime contractor and contract management partner. We focus on compliance, subcontractor oversight, and delivering quality results while ensuring all federal requirements are met.

Our Model

  • Act as prime contractor on awarded federal contracts
  • Engage qualified subcontractors for specialized technical/labor needs
  • Ensure FAR/DFARS and SBA small-business rules compliance
  • Maintain oversight, reporting, and communications with the Government

Payment Terms

Subcontractors will invoice Reeds Solutions, LLC monthly for approved work performed. Reed Solutions will remit payment within thirty (30) days of receiving payment from the Government, less agreed-upon management/administrative fees.

Why Partner With Us

  • Transparent payment terms
  • Compliance-driven processes
  • Efficient subcontractor onboarding
  • Dedicated to building long-term partnerships
