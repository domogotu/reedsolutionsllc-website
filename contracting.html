Our Contracting Expertise

Reed's Solutions LLC provides expert federal contracting services, supporting government agencies and prime contractors with compliant, scalable, and efficient solutions across multiple NAICS codes.

  • Federal Contract Management (NAICS 541611): Comprehensive oversight and execution of government contracts, including program planning, execution oversight, and stakeholder coordination.
  • Computer Systems Design Support (NAICS 541512): System requirements analysis, technical coordination, IT program execution support, and quality assurance.
  • IT Operations & Facilities Management (NAICS 541513): IT operations coordination, service delivery monitoring, compliance tracking, and cross-team communication.
  • Technical Program Support (NAICS 541519): Specialized IT and technical support, cross-functional integration assistance, and mission support in RDT&E-adjacent environments.
  • Management Consulting (NAICS 541618): Strategic and operational analysis, process assessment, and advisory support aligned to mission outcomes.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Ensuring all projects meet FAR/DFARS standards and federal guidelines throughout the contract lifecycle.

How We Work

Reed's Solutions LLC partners with prime contractors and government agencies to understand objectives, implement scalable solutions, and ensure projects are delivered on time and within budget. We are experienced in operating as a low-risk subcontractor, integrating cleanly into prime-led teams without adding management overhead.
