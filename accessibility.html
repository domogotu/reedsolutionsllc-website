Accessibility Statement

Effective date: September 29, 2025

Our Commitment

Reeds Solutions, LLC is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all visitors, including people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone, and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Standards

Our goal is to align with:

  • Section 508 of the U.S. Rehabilitation Act
  • Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA

We use accessibility best practices in design, coding, and testing to ensure our website works with assistive technologies.

Measures Taken

  • Keyboard navigability for all menus and forms
  • Text alternatives for non-text content (images, icons)
  • High contrast and scalable fonts
  • ARIA labels for screen reader compatibility
  • Regular testing with accessibility tools and validators

Limitations & Alternatives

While we aim for full compliance, some third-party content or integrations may not fully meet accessibility standards. If you encounter barriers, we will provide an alternative accessible format upon request.

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our site. If you experience any difficulty, please contact us:

  • Email: Reedssolutionsllcorg@gmail.com
  • Website: reedssolutionsllc.org

Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We regularly review feedback, audit our pages, and train our team to ensure accessibility considerations are built into every update.
