Accessibility Statement

Reeds Solutions, LLC is committed to making our website accessible to all users, including individuals with disabilities.

Our Commitment

We strive to ensure that our website is usable by people of all abilities and follows best practices for accessibility, including WCAG 2.1 guidelines.

Accessibility Features

  • Semantic HTML5 structure with clear headings and landmarks
  • Alt text provided for all images
  • Keyboard navigable menus and forms
  • High contrast text and color options
  • Responsive layout for mobile and tablet devices
  • Accessible forms with labels and ARIA attributes

Feedback

If you experience any accessibility barriers, please contact us at Reedssolutionsllcorg@gmail.com so we can address the issue promptly.

Compliance

Our website is designed to comply with ADA and WCAG 2.1 AA standards wherever possible. We continually review and improve accessibility features.
