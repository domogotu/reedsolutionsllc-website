Accessibility Statement

Reeds Solutions, LLC is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people of all abilities and disabilities.

Commitment to Accessibility

We strive to make our website usable and navigable for everyone, including individuals with visual, auditory, motor, and cognitive disabilities.

Standards and Guidelines

Our website follows the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 and incorporates best practices to meet accessibility standards.

Features

  • Keyboard navigable menus and forms
  • Semantic HTML structure with ARIA roles
  • Alt text on all images and meaningful link descriptions
  • High contrast and readable fonts
  • Responsive design for mobile and desktop devices

Feedback

If you encounter any accessibility barriers while using our website, please contact us at Reedssolutionsllcorg@gmail.com so we can address your concerns.

Ongoing Improvements

We continually review and improve our website to maintain and enhance accessibility for all users.
