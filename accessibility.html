Accessibility Statement

Effective date: September 29, 2025

Our Commitment

We are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all visitors, including people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply relevant accessibility standards.

Standards

  • Section 508 of the U.S. Rehabilitation Act
  • Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA

Measures Taken

  • Keyboard navigability
  • Text alternatives for images
  • High contrast and scalable fonts
  • ARIA labels for screen readers
  • Regular accessibility testing
