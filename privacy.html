Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 29, 2025

Reeds Solutions, LLC (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. This Policy applies to reedssolutionsllc.org and any pages that link to it.

Information We Collect

1) Information You Provide

  • Contact forms: name, email, phone, company/agency, and project details you choose to share.
  • Email or direct contact: any information you send to our inboxes.
  • Applicants (Careers): name, contact details, résumé, and optional PDFs (e.g., certifications, references, cover letter).

2) Information Collected Automatically

Like most websites, we may collect limited technical data (e.g., browser type, device, approximate location derived from IP, referring pages, and pages visited) for security, performance, and analytics.

3) Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

  • Sensitive identifiers (e.g., Social Security Numbers) or protected health information.
  • Payment card data (we do not process payments on this site).
  • Data from children under 13.

If you believe sensitive information was sent to us in error, please contact us so we can delete it.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries, RFQs, and teaming requests.
  • To evaluate applicants for open or potential roles.
  • To operate, secure, and improve our website and services.
  • To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and contract requirements.

Applicants (Careers) — How Your Data Is Handled

If you submit an application via our Careers form, your documents (e.g., résumé, certifications, references, cover letter) are transmitted to us by email through our email provider (e.g., SendGrid). We use this information solely to evaluate your candidacy and to communicate with you about potential opportunities. We do not sell applicant information and we retain it only as long as necessary for recruiting and compliance purposes.

Please do not upload documents containing Social Security Numbers, full DOB, or other highly sensitive personal data.

Legal Basis (where applicable)

We process personal data based on one or more of the following: your consent; our legitimate interests in responding to your inquiries and operating our business; performance of a contract or pre-contractual steps; and compliance with legal obligations.

Sharing of Information

  • Vendors/Service Providers: we may share limited data with trusted providers who help us operate the website, email forms, and applicant intake (e.g., hosting, email delivery).
  • Legal/Compliance: when required by law, regulation, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • No Sale of Personal Information: we do not sell your personal information.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this Policy, including business, legal, or compliance requirements (e.g., contract records, recruiting), after which we delete or de-identify it.

Security

We employ reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; please avoid sending highly sensitive data through public web forms.

Your Choices & Rights

  • Contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your information.
  • Opt out of marketing by following unsubscribe instructions or contacting us.
  • Cookies/Tracking: you can control cookies via your browser settings.

Cookies & Similar Technologies

We may use essential cookies for site functionality and security. If analytics are enabled, they help us understand site usage to improve content and performance. You can manage cookies in your browser settings.

Children’s Privacy

Our site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

International Visitors

Our site is operated in the United States. If you access it from outside the U.S., you understand that your information may be processed in the U.S. where privacy laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective date” above reflects the latest version. Material changes will be posted on this page.
