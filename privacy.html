Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 29, 2025

Reeds Solutions, LLC (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. This Policy applies to reedssolutionsllc.org.

Information We Collect

1) Information You Provide

  • Contact forms: name, email, phone, and project details you provide.
  • Email or direct contact: any information you send us.
  • Applicants (Careers): résumé, cover letter, certifications, references, and contact info.

2) Information Collected Automatically

Basic technical data such as IP, browser, device, referring page, and usage logs.

3) Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

  • SSNs or financial account numbers
  • Protected health information
  • Children’s data under 13

How We Use Information

  • Respond to inquiries and teaming requests
  • Evaluate applicants
  • Operate and improve our website
  • Meet legal and regulatory requirements

Applicants (Careers)

If you submit an application, your documents are emailed securely to us through SendGrid. We use this data only for recruiting and retain it only as long as necessary. Do not upload sensitive identifiers like SSNs.

Sharing of Information

  • Trusted service providers (e.g., hosting, email delivery)
  • Legal or compliance purposes
  • Never sold to third parties

Data Retention & Security

Data is retained only as long as required for business, recruiting, or compliance needs. We use reasonable safeguards to protect it but cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing emails
  • Control cookies in your browser
