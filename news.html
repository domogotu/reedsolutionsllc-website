PrimeContractorOS Now Available to Government Contractors

May 2025

Reed's Solutions LLC is proud to announce the launch of PrimeContractorOS — a purpose-built operating system for government contractors. PrimeContractorOS guides contractors through the full lifecycle, from opportunity identification to contract closeout, with built-in FAR compliance tools, AI-powered guidance, and integrated financial tracking. Available now at Starter, Growth, and Advanced plans.

October 2024

We launched the Federal Pathways Initiative, providing structured educational workshops for entrepreneurs and small businesses to successfully navigate federal contracting opportunities. The initiative covers SAM.gov registration, NAICS code selection, proposal writing, and teaming strategies.
