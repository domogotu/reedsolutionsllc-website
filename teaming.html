Teaming & Partnerships

Teaming Opportunities

Reeds Solutions LLC is open to responsible teaming arrangements with:

Prime Contractors

Teaming as a subcontractor or specialized service provider on prime contractor opportunities.

Subcontractors & Vendors

Coordinating with subcontractors and vendors to deliver comprehensive solutions.

Technology Partners

Partnering with technology providers and software vendors for platform integration and support.

Government Contracting Advisors

Collaborating with advisors, consultants, and subject matter experts.

Teaming Review Standards

When evaluating teaming opportunities, Reeds Solutions LLC reviews:

  • Scope Review: Clear definition of scope, responsibilities, and deliverables
  • Flowdown Review: Understanding of contract flowdown requirements and compliance obligations
  • Payment Terms Review: Clear payment terms, invoicing procedures, and financial arrangements
  • Source Document Review: Verification of source documents, contract terms, and compliance requirements
  • Insurance & Licensing Review: Verification of required insurance, licenses, and certifications where applicable

How to Submit a Teaming Inquiry

To propose a teaming arrangement with Reeds Solutions LLC, please include the following information in your inquiry:

  • Your company name and contracting status (prime, subcontractor, vendor, etc.)
  • Opportunity overview (agency, contract type, estimated value)
  • Proposed scope for Reeds Solutions LLC
  • Timeline and key milestones
  • Proposed teaming structure and payment terms
  • Any relevant RFP, solicitation, or opportunity documentation

Teaming Inquiry Response Time

Reeds Solutions LLC typically responds to teaming inquiries within 2-3 business days. For time-sensitive opportunities, please note the deadline in your inquiry.
