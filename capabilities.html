Capabilities

Federal IT & Facilities services aligned to your mission, delivered with compliance and accountability.

IT & Technology Services

We manage and support the technical backbone of federal agencies, ensuring mission-critical systems remain secure, resilient, and fully operational.

Computer Facilities Management

End-to-end management of data centers and IT environments, including server uptime, patching, monitoring, and lifecycle support. We safeguard against downtime and ensure performance under federal security standards.

NAICS 541513 (Primary)

Systems Design & Integration

We help agencies design, build, and deploy integrated systems that connect hardware, software, and networks seamlessly. From initial architecture to final deployment, we ensure smooth implementation with minimal disruption.

NAICS 541512

Other Computer Related Services

Comprehensive IT support services such as help desk, hardware repair, asset management, and remote troubleshooting. We act as a one-stop shop for agencies to reduce complexity and improve response times.

NAICS 541519

Facilities & Operations

Our facilities management capabilities cover everything from day-to-day maintenance to specialized procurement, ensuring federal operations are efficient, safe, and mission-ready.

Facilities Management

We provide both preventive and corrective maintenance programs for buildings, equipment, and critical infrastructure. By partnering with vetted subcontractors, we ensure compliance with safety, environmental, and performance standards.

NAICS 561210

Specialized Procurement

We source and deliver mission-essential technologies and machinery, such as IT hardware, 3D printers, and specialized industry equipment. Our procurement expertise ensures agencies receive the right tools, on time and within budget.

NAICS 532490 + related PSCs

Logistics & Program Support

We provide site planning, inventory control, and logistics support for large-scale projects. From mobilization to close-out, we keep operations on track and aligned with mission requirements.

Contract Oversight & Compliance

Our contract management expertise ensures all projects remain compliant with federal regulations while meeting performance goals. We oversee subcontractors, documentation, and audits, providing peace of mind to contracting officers.

Subcontractor Management

We onboard, manage, and oversee subcontractors to ensure performance standards are met. This includes monitoring deliverables, handling compliance paperwork, and resolving performance issues quickly.

FAR/DFARS Compliance

We interpret, implement, and enforce federal regulations (FAR/DFARS) across our contracts. Our compliance team ensures all requirements are met, protecting both the agency and subcontractors from risk.

Quality & Performance

Through KPIs, SLAs, and regular audits, we track contract performance and implement continuous improvement. This ensures each project meets or exceeds mission outcomes and federal expectations.
