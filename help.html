Frequently Asked Questions

What is PrimeContractorOS? +

PrimeContractorOS is a government-contracting operations platform designed to help contractors manage the full contracting lifecycle — from opportunity identification through proposal, awarded contract, active operations, invoicing, payments, closeout, and lessons learned.

Who is PrimeContractorOS for? +

PrimeContractorOS is designed for businesses acting as prime contractors, subcontractors, or both. It supports opportunity tracking, proposal management, contract administration, subcontractor coordination, and financial tracking for government-contracting work.

How do I get started? +

Visit our Get Started page and submit your information. Our team will follow up during business hours to help you set up your workspace and get access to PrimeContractorOS.

What is a workspace? +

A workspace is your dedicated PrimeContractorOS environment where you manage your government-contracting records, opportunities, proposals, contracts, invoices, payments, and team members.

Does PrimeContractorOS replace legal counsel? +

No. PrimeContractorOS is a workflow and organization tool. It does not replace legal counsel, contracting officer direction, certified accounting advice, or final compliance review. Always consult with appropriate professionals for legal and compliance matters.
