Reeds Solutions LLC is actively building our network of qualified professionals, subcontractors, and partners. We are interested in connecting with talented individuals and organizations who share our commitment to government contracting excellence and innovation.

Opportunities We're Exploring

Full-Time Positions

As we grow, we will be seeking talented professionals in areas such as:

  • Government Contracting Operations
  • Software Development & Engineering
  • Product Management
  • Business Development
  • Compliance & Regulatory Affairs
  • Customer Success & Support

Subcontractor & Vendor Relationships

We are open to subcontracting arrangements with qualified professionals and organizations in complementary areas such as:

  • IT Services & Technology Support
  • Facilities Support & Coordination
  • Government Contracting Consulting
  • Legal & Compliance Services
  • Marketing & Communications

Technology & Strategic Partnerships

We welcome partnerships with technology providers, integrators, and strategic advisors who can enhance our capabilities and expand our reach in the government contracting market.

What We're Looking For

  • Passion for government contracting and federal operations
  • Strong attention to detail and compliance awareness
  • Collaborative team mindset
  • Commitment to professional excellence
  • Experience or interest in technology and innovation
  • Alignment with our values of transparency and integrity

Company Culture

Reeds Solutions LLC is a founder-led, minority-owned business focused on building a professional, inclusive, and innovative workplace. We value:

  • Professional execution and attention to detail
  • Clear communication and transparency

  • Integrity in all business dealings
  • Diversity and inclusion
