PrimeContractorOS Features

Comprehensive tools designed for government contractors to manage the full contracting lifecycle.

Core Features

Opportunity Tracking

Identify, track, and manage government contracting opportunities from discovery through proposal submission. Organize opportunities by status, deadline, and priority.

Proposal Management

Organize proposal requirements, track deliverables, manage team assignments, and maintain version control throughout the proposal development process.

Contract Administration

Store and manage contract documents, track key dates, monitor compliance requirements, and maintain organized contract records throughout the contract lifecycle.

Subcontractor Coordination

Coordinate with subcontractors, track teaming relationships, manage flowdown requirements, and maintain clear communication channels.

Invoice & Payment Tracking

Track invoices, monitor payment status, manage billing cycles, and maintain financial records for government contracts.

File Storage & Organization

Centralize document storage with secure file management, version control, and easy retrieval for all contract-related documents.

Advanced Features

Guidance AI

Get AI-assisted recommendations for setup, process optimization, and best practices for government contracting operations.

Source-Linked Analysis

AI-powered contract review that identifies key terms, compliance requirements, and potential issues with source-linked analysis.

Task Management

Create, assign, and track tasks related to opportunities, proposals, contracts, and ongoing operations.

Team Collaboration

Invite team members to your workspace, assign roles, and collaborate on opportunities and contracts.

Reporting & Analytics

Generate reports on pipeline status, proposal performance, contract metrics, and business development progress.

Compliance Tracking

Track compliance requirements, monitor deadlines, and maintain records of compliance-related activities.

Workflow Features

  • Opportunity Lifecycle: From discovery and qualification through proposal, award, and closeout
  • Contract Lifecycle: From award through active operations, invoicing, and final closeout
  • Lessons Learned: Document and track lessons learned for continuous improvement
  • Historical Records: Maintain complete historical records for past performance and capability statements
  • Integration Ready: Designed to work with your existing tools and workflows

Security & Compliance

PrimeContractorOS is built with security and compliance in mind:

  • Secure data storage and encryption
  • Role-based access control
  • Audit trails and activity logging
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for data in transit
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Compliance with applicable data protection regulations
