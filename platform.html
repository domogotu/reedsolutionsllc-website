PrimeContractorOS Platform

The complete operating system for government contractors. Manage your full contracting lifecycle — from opportunity to closeout — with AI-powered guidance built in.

Platform Status

PrimeContractorOS is currently in active development and commercialization preparation. Some features may be staged, under refinement, or dependent on future integrations, testing, subscriptions, or customer onboarding.

Who This Is For

New Contractors

First-time federal contractors navigating the complexity of SAM registration, opportunity identification, and proposal development.

Active Contractors

Established contractors managing multiple opportunities, proposals, and active contracts simultaneously.

Subcontractors

Subcontractors coordinating with prime contractors, tracking flowdown requirements, and managing performance obligations.

Teaming Partners

Organizations pursuing teaming arrangements and coordinating across multiple entities and compliance domains.

Core Workflow Features

🎯 Opportunity Tracking

Track federal opportunities from SAM.gov with smart intake, duplicate detection, and auto-fill. Organize opportunities by status, timeline, and strategic fit.

📄 Proposal Management

Build and manage proposals with templates, collaboration tools, AI-assisted review, and win/loss tracking. Track proposal status from draft through award.

📋 Contract Administration

Manage active contracts with built-in FAR/DFARS compliance guidance, clause extraction, and deliverable tracking. Monitor contract performance and compliance.

✅ FAR 4.804 Closeout

Complete contract closeout with a guided checklist aligned to FAR 4.804 requirements. Ensure all closeout documentation is complete and compliant.

AI & Intelligence Features

🤖 AI-Powered Guidance

Get intelligent recommendations, compliance alerts, contract scanning, and source-linked findings. AI guidance is designed to support, not replace, professional judgment.

📚 70,000-Answer Guidance Engine

Structured guidance covering every contracting question across six dimensions — Who, What, When, Where, Why, and How. Comprehensive reference for government contracting.

🔍 Smart Search & Command Center

Three-layer search plus Cmd+K spotlight for universal quick actions and keyboard shortcuts. Find what you need instantly across the entire platform.

⚖️ Rate Parity Testing

Cross-system rate verification across proposals, contracts, invoices, and payments. Ensure rate consistency and accuracy throughout the contract lifecycle.

Finance & Operations Features

💰 Finance & Invoicing

Generate invoices, track payments, and monitor financial performance across all your contracts. Maintain accurate financial records and payment tracking.

👥 Team Collaboration

Invite team members, assign roles, manage subcontractors, and collaborate with role-based access control. Organize your team and manage permissions.

📥 Smart Intake & SAM.gov Import

Four creation paths — manual entry, SAM.gov import, template-based, or AI-assisted. Flexible opportunity intake for your workflow.

📈 Reports & Analytics

Generate financial summaries, win/loss analyses, and compliance reports. Export to PDF for presentations and record-keeping.

Additional Features

  • Files, Contacts, and Messages — Centralized document and communication management
  • Prime/Subcontractor Operations — Coordinate across multiple organizational levels
  • Rule-Aware Contracting Support — Compliance guidance throughout the platform
  • Platform Owner Controls — Manage settings, users, and organizational preferences
  • Source-Linked Analysis Support — Contract review with source document linking
