Security & Compliance

Reeds Solutions LLC maintains security and compliance standards for all operations.

Security Commitment

Reeds Solutions LLC is committed to maintaining robust security practices and compliance with applicable regulations and standards. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect sensitive information and maintain operational integrity.

Compliance Standards

We maintain awareness of and compliance with the following standards and regulations:

  • Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) requirements
  • Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement (DFARS) requirements
  • Agency-specific compliance requirements
  • Data protection and privacy regulations
  • Cybersecurity best practices
  • Business continuity and disaster recovery standards

Data Security

We implement appropriate administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect data and information systems:

  • Access controls and authentication
  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security assessments and audits
  • Incident response procedures
  • Employee security training and awareness
  • Vendor and third-party security management

Government Contracting Compliance

For government contracting work, we maintain compliance with:

  • SAM.gov registration and maintenance
  • Socioeconomic status certifications
  • Contract compliance requirements
  • Flowdown compliance for subcontractors
  • Invoice and payment documentation requirements
  • Audit and inspection readiness

Continuous Improvement

We continuously review and improve our security and compliance practices through:

  • Regular security assessments
  • Compliance audits and reviews
  • Employee training and awareness programs
  • Incident reporting and analysis
  • Vendor security evaluations
  • Industry best practice adoption

Questions or Concerns

If you have questions about our security and compliance practices, please contact us.
