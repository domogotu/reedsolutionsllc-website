About Reeds Solutions, LLC

Changing the game in federal IT and facilities contracting through innovation, compliance, and trusted partnerships.

Who We Are

Reeds Solutions, LLC is a California-based small business dedicated to transforming federal IT and facilities contracting. We manage complex contracts, ensure compliance with FAR/DFARS, and coordinate specialized subcontractors to deliver mission-critical outcomes efficiently and safely.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support federal agencies with compliant, innovative, and reliable solutions that advance mission success, strengthen oversight, and deliver measurable results.

Our Vision

We envision a future where small businesses set the standard for federal contracting. By leveraging technology, strong subcontractor networks, and a compliance-first approach, Reeds Solutions, LLC delivers results that move agencies beyond traditional limitations and toward long-term mission success.
