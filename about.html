About Reeds Solutions LLC

Who We Are

Reeds Solutions LLC is a California-based professional business focused on government contracting, IT services, facilities support, and operational coordination. We support federal contracting readiness, capability alignment, subcontractor coordination, compliance-aware operations, and project execution support.

Reeds Solutions LLC is an early-stage, pre-revenue business and is currently in product development stage. We are SAM registered with UEI L5W1JWUUDMW3 and CAGE 157R9, and we are actively preparing for prime, subcontractor, teaming, technology, and facilities-support opportunities.

Founder & Leadership

Reeds Solutions LLC is founder-led by Dominique La'Curtis Reed, a California-based entrepreneur focused on building innovative solutions for government contractors and federal operations.

How We Work

  1. Review Requirements: We carefully review project scope, contract terms, and client expectations.
  2. Align Capabilities: We assess our capabilities and identify qualified partners or subcontractors as needed.
  3. Coordinate Resources: We organize resources, establish clear communication channels, and define responsibilities.
  4. Support Documentation: We maintain organized, compliant documentation throughout the project lifecycle.
  5. Track Work to Completion: We monitor progress, track deliverables, and ensure timely completion and closeout.

Prime and Subcontracting Support

Reeds Solutions LLC may support work as a prime contractor, subcontractor, or teaming partner depending on the opportunity and scope. We coordinate with qualified subcontractors and partners where appropriate to deliver comprehensive solutions.
