About Reeds Solutions, LLC

Changing the game in federal IT and facilities contracting — with agility, accountability, and trusted teaming.

Who We Are

Reeds Solutions, LLC is a California‑based small business focused on helping federal agencies execute mission‑critical work in IT and facilities. We coordinate specialized subcontractors, oversee performance, and ensure each task is delivered on time, compliant, and aligned to objectives.

Our model is simple: assemble the right talent for each requirement, manage the work with discipline, and remove friction between planning and execution.

Our Mission

Deliver practical, reliable solutions that move federal programs forward — elevating quality, transparency, and outcomes on every contract.

Agile & Scalable
