About Reeds Solutions, LLC

Changing the game in federal IT and facilities contracting through innovation, compliance, and trusted partnerships.

Who We Are

Reeds Solutions, LLC is a California-based small business dedicated to transforming federal IT and facilities contracting. We don’t believe in business as usual — we create smarter, more efficient ways to deliver government projects. With compliance as our foundation and innovation as our driver, we are the partner agencies can count on to get the job done right, the first time.

Our Mission

Our mission is to change the game in government contracting by combining innovation, compliance, and trusted partnerships to deliver reliable, forward-looking solutions for every agency we serve.

Our Vision

We envision a future where small businesses set the standard for federal contracting. By leveraging technology, strong subcontractor networks, and a compliance-first approach, Reeds Solutions, LLC delivers results that move agencies beyond traditional limitations and toward long-term mission success.

Core Values

Integrity
